Sydney Project Management Group provides services and leadership to its members, and the Project Management community, through access to opportunities that expand and strengthen professional, personal and organizational goals and objectives.

Membership in SPMG is open to all eligible individuals without regard to race, creed, color, age, sex, sexual orientation, marital status, national origin, religion or disability.

Our mission:

  • Professional Development: Deliver Education Programs, Promote Certification, and offer Leadership Opportunities for Volunteers
  • Outreach: establish Corporate partnerships, initiate and participate in Community Service activities, and develop Academic relationships
  • Member Value: Developing & Retaining Volunteers and Thought Leaders, and Recognizing Professional Achievements
  • Marketing: Build Awareness of SPMG Brand through Internal and External Campaigns and Sponsorships
  • Communications: Facilitate dissemination of information related to the Project Management profession in the Sydney area
